# Evaluator Output Contract

The evaluator may produce internal accuracy and learning artifacts after the blind ledger is sealed.

## Internal Outputs

- Seal verification result.
- Label inventory.
- Stable-ID join audit.
- Metrics table.
- Confusion matrix.
- Residual error essay.
- Signal promotion notes.
- Regression-test recommendations.

## Public Outputs

Public deliverables should remain limited and readable:

- Client workbook with original rows and appended review columns.
- Client narrative report explaining findings, discard recommendations, protective guardrails, and next-pass learning.

The evaluator must not create a public pile of intermediate files that confuse the client.
